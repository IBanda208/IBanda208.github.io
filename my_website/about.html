About Banda

So during the summer I went to Mammoth for a week. Part of the cross country team goes for alttiude training every summer, and this was my second summer going. We go on runs twice a day, and the first run we do is to Sherwin Lake. It has a semi steep uphill, but coming back down the mountain is super difficult because of all the rocks. So on the way back down I tripped and dove face first into a rock. It was on the first day, a monday, and we weren't going to leave till satruday. So I ended up spending the week afraid of rocks. Here's another short story about me hitting my face. I was at the fair on Sunday, the 19th, and I got on the zipper ride. I was told to hold on, but of course I didn't listen. So as soon as the cage flipped around, my face slammed right into it. All in all, I am a clumsy person who hits her face alot.
